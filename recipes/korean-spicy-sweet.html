Korean Spicy Sweet Tofu

Description

Crispy, spicy, sweet, tangy, delicious! And simple!

This recipe relies on combining three sauces, heating them up, and that's it. You probably already have one or two of the sauces in your fridge, and the others can easily be found at any supermarket.

Ingredients

  • Ketchup - 1/3 cup
  • Gochujang - 1/3 cup
  • Rice Syrup (or simple syrup!) - 1/3 cup
  • Conor's EZ Tofu

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Prepare the tofu, set aside
  2. Add ketchup, gochujang, and syrup together in a frying pan on medium-low heat
  3. Heat sauce until gochujang loosens and begins to bubble. The sauce should be easy to stir together. If it is still too thick to mix well, add a splash of water until it is
  4. Mix in tofu and stir until combined. Serve immediately.
