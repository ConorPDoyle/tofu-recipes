Hawaiian Tofu

Description

This simple recipe will make you lose your mind with how simple and easy it is. It is about as complicated as making store-bought spaghetti: heat sauce, add to main dish.

For this recipe, I recommend Halm's Hawaiian BBQ sauce, as it is the perfect flavor profile we're looking for. Sugary, soy saucy, miso-y, sesame-y. Delishy.

Ingredients

  • Halm's Hawaiian Bar-B-Que Sauce - 1 cup
  • Conor's EZ Tofu
  • OPTIONAL - Green onion, sliced (to garnish)
  • OPTIONAL - Kewpie Mayonnaise (to garnish)

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Prepare the tofu, set aside
  2. Add Halm's to sauce pot on medium-low heat
  3. Heat sauce until warm. DO NOT OVERHEAT - the sugar in the sauce can very easily burn.
  4. Mix in tofu and stir until combined. Add optional garnishes and serve immediately.
