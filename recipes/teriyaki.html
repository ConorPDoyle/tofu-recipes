Teriyaki Tofu

Description

Delicious recipe that can be modified however you want. Great with frozen vegetables, fresh vegetables. Add frozen broccoli for a healthy meal. Use noodles, rice, doesn't matter. A great Seattle classic.

Ingredients

  • Conor's EZ Tofu
  • Your favorite teriyaki sauce - 1 cup
  • Corn starch slurry
    • 2 tsp water
    • 2 tsp corn starch, swirled in
  • OPTIONAL - Sesame seeds (to garnish)
  • OPTIONAL - Sliced onion, sliced (to garnish)

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Prepare the tofu, set aside
  2. Add teriyaki sauce to sauce pot on medium heat.
  3. Heat sauce until it just begins to bubble. Add corn starch slurry into simmering sauce to thicken it.
  4. Mix in tofu and stir until combined. Add optional garnishes and serve immediately.
